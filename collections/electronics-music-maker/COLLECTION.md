# Electronics & Music Maker / Music Technology

UK electronics and music technology magazine. Published as *Electronics & Music Maker*
from 1981 until 1986, then relaunched as *Music Technology* through to 1994. Strong
coverage of synthesisers, studio electronics, MIDI, and DIY music hardware throughout.
